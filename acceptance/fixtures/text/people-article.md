Tech Conference Recap: Innovations in Distributed Systems

On March 12, 2024, Alice Chen opened the morning keynote in Berlin with a presentation on consensus algorithms. She argued that the industry has underestimated the cost of leader election in high-churn clusters.

Bob Martinez, a principal engineer at Infracore, followed up on April 3 in Tokyo with a live demonstration of his team's custom Raft implementation. He highlighted three edge cases that had caused production incidents in the previous quarter.

The afternoon panel in Berlin was chaired by Priya Nair, who moderated a discussion on observability tooling. She invited David Kim to present his latency-profiling framework, which the Seoul office had been piloting since January 15, 2024.

Key dates and locations covered in this article:
- March 12, 2024 — Berlin (keynote by Alice Chen)
- April 3, 2024 — Tokyo (demo by Bob Martinez)
- January 15, 2024 — Seoul pilot start (David Kim's framework)
- Panel discussion also held in Berlin, chaired by Priya Nair
