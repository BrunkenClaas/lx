Rules:
- type: choose the best-fit Conventional Commits type
- scope: short noun describing what area changed (e.g. "auth", "api", "cli"); leave empty if not obvious

- body: 1-3 sentences explaining WHY the change was made; empty string if trivial
- Do NOT include file names in the subject unless essential for clarity
- Reply in {lang}. If you cannot reliably produce output in {lang}, reply in English and prepend your response with [lang-fallback].
